From designing the classroom to the interface.

I worked in education, meeting learners from many different backgrounds and learning styles. That experience taught me how to listen, observe behavior, and simplify complexity for people.

While helping others discover themselves, I was quietly searching for my own path too.

In 2023, I discovered UX/UI design — and suddenly, everything connected. I started as making learning experiences for students turned into making interfaces for users. The tools were different but my approach is the same: watch carefully, talk clearly, and make experiences that really help people.

Now, I'm a UX/UI Designer based in Bangkok, I work on products the same way I taught with kindness organization and thinking about the people, on the other side of the screen. I still design every screen like I'm explaining it to someone for the first time.
